Cultivation Story

Grown in darkness,
finished in gold.

Noctis Aurum spends eleven months sealed in volcanic shade before its first bud appears. Our growers feed it nothing but mineral-rich condensation drawn from the mountain fog — the same trace minerals that later surface as its signature gold veining.

Once cut, a bloom survives four days at most. So instead, we bring the room to the flower: every AURUM piece is viewed once, by appointment, inside a single darkened chamber lit only by the orchid itself.

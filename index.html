ABOUT

Two crafts, one instinct.

I'm Aayush — a Discord bot developer and graphic designer who treats every brand and every bot the same way: as a first impression that has to earn trust in seconds.

On one side, I design — logos, banners, and full brand identities for local businesses, budget brands, startups, and large-scale companies alike. On the other, I build — custom Discord bots that give communities a personality of their own. Most days I'm doing both at once, because the two feed each other: good design makes a bot feel alive, and good engineering makes a brand feel reliable.
